# 01 - Pricing Process

## Business Model Overview
The Pricing Process encompasses all activities related to determining, calculating, and managing product and service prices within the organization. This process includes pricing strategy development, calculation methodologies, approval workflows, and ongoing price management activities.

## Requirements Source
Requirements for this process are driven by:
- Business pricing strategy objectives
- Market competitive requirements
- Regulatory and compliance constraints  
- Operational efficiency goals
- Financial performance targets
- Customer experience requirements

## Process Scope
This process covers pricing activities at the organizational level including:
- Pricing strategy formulation and policies
- Price calculation and modeling
- Pricing approval workflows
- Price change management
- Pricing analytics and reporting
- Integration with sales and finance systems

## Business Context
Effective pricing is critical for:
- Revenue optimization and profitability
- Market competitiveness
- Customer satisfaction and retention
- Regulatory compliance
- Operational efficiency
- Strategic business objectives

## Key Stakeholders
- **Sales Teams**: Price execution and customer interaction
- **Finance Department**: Financial analysis and approval
- **Product Management**: Product pricing strategy
- **Marketing**: Pricing strategy and positioning
- **Executive Leadership**: Strategic pricing decisions
- **Customers**: Price transparency and value perception
- **Regulatory Bodies**: Compliance requirements
